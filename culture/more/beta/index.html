Learning about Beta
What does beta mean?

Beta defines the state of which where an organization or related are in developmental stages pre-finalization. Basically, we're still making sure we can do better before we start publicly issuing our website to people like you!


What does that mean for the website?

Something you see or read one day may change the very next; we're constantly updating our website to make it more informative and useful, sticking to our core objective of providing a safe and detailed website for portfolios and package sharing. So, you cannot access the full potential of our website, but you will soon.


Post-Beta Stages
What comes after beta?

After beta will be alpha, where we will start integrating a large amount of users to our website, however will still not be public; beta still has users, but a very small and select quantity of them.


When are you publicly releasing?

Very soon! At least within the next week or two, so keep checking back to get involved!
