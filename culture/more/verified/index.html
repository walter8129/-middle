What is Verified?

Verified is a tag awarded to sources, packages or users which ensures the viewer of their safety, security and legitimacy. We award these tags directly, so anything with this tag has been checked!


Can I always trust Verification?

Being verified simply means that this source, package or user has passed an assessment which ensures their legitimacy, however we can not regularly monitor all verified sources, packages and users, so although they may be verified, it may have accidentally changed making it unreliable, or might have illicitly changed - this is, however, assessed at the start, and verified tags will eventually be rechecked by a member of staff!


How can I keep safe?

Verified tags provide a good foundation of ensuring you are safe, and Pixel will never send you to an illicit URL, all verified tags lead to secure links and are not made or controlled by external individuals. With this in mind, it is always important to run your eye over all contents before downloading or employing, because verified or not, Pixel cannot mind read and nor can you!
